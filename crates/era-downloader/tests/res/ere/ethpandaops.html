Ethereum Mainnet Execution Layer Archive

This repository contains the complete Ethereum Mainnet execution layer history in eraE format, from genesis through both the pre-merge (PoW) and post-merge (PoS) eras. These files enable historical inspection, client bootstrapping, and independent verification of Ethereum Mainnet's execution layer chain.
